1. **Window Size**: The window size is `size(400, 300)`.
2. **Dice for Person A**: Person A's dice is set to roll numbers from 1 to 6 (`(int)random(1, 7)`).
3. **Dice for Person B**: Person B's dice is set to roll numbers from 1 to 4 (`(int)random(1, 5)`).
4. **Standard Output for Results**: Determines the winner based on the larger value, or a draw if equal, and outputs the result.
   - Example Output:
     - Person A: 3
     - Person B: 4
     - Person B wins
5. **Click Implementation**: Re-rolls the dice and updates the result with each mouse click.
